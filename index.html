Your Questions,
Warmly Answered

No confusion. No stress. Honest answers from people who know Vietnam and India both.

Will we find vegetarian food easily in Vietnam?

+

Absolutely. Vietnam has excellent vegetarian options — and VIETANA specifically guides you to the best veg-friendly restaurants. The Spicy Spoon in HCMC is our recommended Indian veg spot. We also know local Vietnamese places with great tofu and vegetable dishes.

Is Vietnam comfortable for Indian travelers?

+

Vietnam is one of the most Indian-friendly destinations in Southeast Asia. People are warm, food is familiar, costs are reasonable and the culture resonates deeply with Indian sensibilities. VIETANA makes it even easier with Hindi-speaking support and expert local knowledge.

Can everything be customized for our family?

+

Yes — completely. We design family trips with safe accommodations, child-friendly activities, easy transport and full dietary consideration. Just tell us your family's preferences and we handle everything around your comfort.

Is local support available if something goes wrong?

+

Yes — always. Chayan Soni and our local HCMC team are available 24/7. Whether it's a hotel issue, transport delay or any local emergency — we respond fast, in person if needed.

How fast is the Vietnam visa processed?

+

We process Vietnam e-visas within 24 hours for most Indian passport holders. We guide you through every document needed, fill the application correctly and ensure your approval before travel.

Can we book just some services, not a full package?

+

Absolutely. Visa only, airport pickup only, hotel only — whatever you need. VIETANA is completely flexible. We're happy to help with one service or your entire trip from start to finish.
